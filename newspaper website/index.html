konak sun temple

The Konark Sun Temple, also known as the Konarak Sun Temple, is a famous Hindu temple dedicated to the sun god Surya. It is located in the town of Konark in the state of Odisha, India. The temple is renowned for its stunning architecture, intricate stone carvings, and its unique design in the shape of a chariot with 24 wheels, pulled by seven horses, representing the sun god's chariot
